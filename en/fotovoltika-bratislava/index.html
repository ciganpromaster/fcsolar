Who administers the subsidies (SIEA)

Direct state subsidies for solar panels are not provided by a private company, but are managed by the Slovak Innovation and Energy Agency (SIEA). Households can use the Green for Households project, while businesses use Green for Businesses. The subsidy significantly reduces your initial investment (up to €4,050 for a household, up to €50,000 for businesses).

Who to contact and certifications

We take complete care of all administration and bureaucracy for you. As your professional partner, we manage everything, including communication with your distributor – ZSD and verifying the reserved connection capacity. With FC Solar, you won't have to deal with any paperwork; we handle all the work for you, requiring only your title deed to get started.

Solutions for companies in the Bratislava region

Commercial solar installations offer a rapid return on investment (typically 6–8 years without subsidies) due to higher business electricity rates. Furthermore, the Green for Businesses program covers the full cost of mandatory professional energy audits.
