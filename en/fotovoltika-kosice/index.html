Who administers the subsidies (SIEA)

Direct state subsidies for solar panels are not provided by a private company, but are managed by the Slovak Innovation and Energy Agency (SIEA). Households can use the Green for Households project, while businesses use Green for Businesses. The subsidy significantly reduces your initial investment (up to €4,050 for a household, up to €50,000 for businesses).

Who to contact and certifications

Contact certified contractors registered with SIEA. Key players in the Kosice region include VSE (Innogy), Antik Telecom. Ensure the partner holds valid electrical engineering certifications. Before signing, the company must verify reserved connection capacity with your distributor - VSD.

Solutions for companies in the Kosice region

Companies save faster due to more expensive electricity (ROI without subsidy: 6-8 years). Through Green for Businesses, they'll also reimburse you for an energy audit.
